Why a reminder beats "correction" for tech neck

Forward head posture — often called tech neck — is when your head drifts forward as you stare at a screen for too long. Plenty of people search for ways to "fix" it, but the truth is that no app or gadget can physically straighten your neck for you. What the latest evidence points to is simple: it's not about holding one "perfect" position, but about not staying in any single position too long and changing it often.

The best posture is — your next one.
